What We Advise On

Independent advice across every stage of your financial life

From planning for retirement to passing wealth on to the next generation, our advice is tailored to your circumstances and reviewed as your life changes.

Investments

Portfolios constructed and reviewed against your goals, time horizon and attitude to risk, using the whole of the market rather than a single provider's range.

Pensions & Retirement

Planning for the income you will need later and reviewing existing pensions, so you understand your options well before you reach retirement.

Protection

Life assurance and business protection arranged to suit your family or company, helping safeguard the people and plans that depend on you.

Inheritance Tax & Estate Planning

Structuring your estate so that more of what you have built can pass to the people and causes you choose, in line with current rules.

Financial Planning

A clear, written plan that brings your savings, investments, pensions and protection together, then keeps them aligned as your circumstances change.

Savings

Advice on tax-efficient ways to put money aside for shorter and longer term goals, reviewed as allowances and your plans evolve.

Genuinely Independent

A father and son practice, answerable only to its clients

Whyte Sharp Independent is directly authorised by the Financial Conduct Authority. We are not an appointed representative of a national network and we are not tied to any single provider's products. That independence means our recommendations start with you, not with a panel we have been asked to sell from.

David Watson has been a partner since the firm's beginning in 1997. His son Kyle joined as a partner in 2020, bringing the practice into its second generation while the same standards of service carry through.

How We Work

A considered, four-step approach

Independent advice is a relationship, not a single transaction. Here is how a conversation with us typically unfolds.

1

Get Acquainted

We start with an unhurried conversation about your circumstances, your plans and what you would like your money to do.

2

Research the Market

As an independent firm we research across the whole of the market to find what genuinely fits, rather than a fixed panel.

3

Set Out a Plan

You receive a clear, written plan explaining what we recommend and the reasoning behind each part of it.

4

Review Together

We meet regularly to review your plan, adjusting it as allowances, markets and your own circumstances change.
